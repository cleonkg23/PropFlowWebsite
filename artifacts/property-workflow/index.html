§ 01 The problem

Most small property teams aren’t short on effort. They’re short on visibility.

  1. i.Enquiries answered in an hour, or in three days — nobody knows which.
  2. ii.Maintenance drifts down the inbox until a tenant chases.
  3. iii.Follow-ups after viewings and landlord requests get quietly forgotten.
  4. iv.One person holds the picture in their head. When they’re off, work stops.
§ 02 — What I do

Six workflows that quietly remove most of the admin from a small property team.

Enquiry triage

Capture enquiries from every channel into one queue, categorised and routed.

Viewing follow-ups

Automatic follow-up tasks after every viewing, with reminders when nothing happens.

Maintenance tracking

Turn a tenant message into a tracked job with owner, status and SLA.

Document chasing

References, ID, deposits and signed agreements — chased on a schedule until returned.

Internal handoffs

The next person gets a clean task — not a forwarded email thread.

Status & pipeline

One shared view of every job, updated automatically as the work moves.
