# 🔌 LOCAL AI IMPLEMENTATION (OLLAMA)

Use:

`gemma4:e4b`

---

## Requirements:

  `http://localhost:11434/api/generate`

* send prompt

* receive response

## Prompt design:

### Classification prompt:

"category": "...",
"urgency": "low|medium|high"

### Draft prompt:
